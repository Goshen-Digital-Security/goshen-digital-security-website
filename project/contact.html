Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start helping us?

For consultations and assessments, we can typically start within 48-72 hours. For ongoing services, we can usually begin within a week of our initial consultation.

Do you work with organizations outside the US?

Yes, we provide remote cybersecurity services to organizations worldwide. Our assessment and training programs are designed to work across different jurisdictions.

What if I'm not sure what services I need?

That's exactly why we offer free consultations. We'll discuss your current situation, identify risks, and recommend the most appropriate services for your needs and budget.

Can you help with compliance requirements?

Yes, we help organizations understand and implement security measures to meet various compliance requirements including GDPR, HIPAA, and industry-specific regulations.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we understand that cash flow is important for small organizations. We offer flexible payment options including monthly plans and project-based pricing.

What makes you different from other cybersecurity firms?

We specialize in serving underserved organizations - small businesses, nonprofits, and solopreneurs. Our approach is human-first, practical, and designed for teams without dedicated IT staff.
